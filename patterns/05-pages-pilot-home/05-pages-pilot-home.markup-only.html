Welcome to the Mass.Gov Pilot

We're redesigning this website to make it easier to find the things you need.

This is a big project, so we’re going public bit by bit, right here where you can see it and be a part of it. We hope you’ll explore the new site and provide some feedback so we can keep improving it as we add more information and features.

Thank you for being a part of this project!

A Message from Governor Baker

“Mass.gov is the digital front door of government — the first place many people in the Commonwealth start their interactions with the state. In our ongoing mission to ensure that state government works better for everyone, we are excited to unveil the new Mass.gov pilot, an innovative testing laboratory to help you get the support you need and the answers you’re looking for — fast and easy.
